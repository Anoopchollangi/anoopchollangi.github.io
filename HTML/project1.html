Electric Two-Wheeler

Design Competition (SAEINDIA)

In this exhilarating competition, we took the reins and embarked on the remarkable journey of crafting our very own Electric Vehicle. Taking on the dual role of leader and designer, I spearheaded this project with passion and innovation. The heart of our creation, the chassis, came to life through my intricate design using the powerful CATIA V5 software. Remarkably, I achieved an impressive feat by meticulously crafting a chassis weighing in at just 6 kilograms. It was a testament to my dedication and ingenuity, pushing the boundaries of what was thought possible. With the foundation set, I seamlessly transitioned into the realm of analysis, employing Ansys 19.2 (Workbench) to scrutinize and refine the chassis design. Through meticulous simulations and meticulous attention to detail, I ensured that our creation would not only be cutting-edge in its design but also excel in performance and safety. This experience has been a testament to the boundless capabilities of teamwork, innovation, and determination. As I reflect on our journey, I am brimming with pride at what we've achieved and excitedly anticipate the impact our exceptional Electric Vehicle will make in the competition.
